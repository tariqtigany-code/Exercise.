Sudanese Agashe

It is very delicious. The meat is so good. It has peanuts and spices.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (cut thin)
  • 1 cup peanut powder
  • 1 spoon ginger
  • Salt and pepper
  • Little oil
  • Onions and lemon

How to make it

  1. Mix the peanut powder and spices in a plate.
  2. Cover the meat with the peanut mix.
  3. Put the meat on sticks.
  4. Grill the meat for 10 minutes.
  5. Eat it hot with onions. Enjoy!
