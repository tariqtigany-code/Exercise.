Overview

Sinners (2025) is a supernatural horror film directed by Ryan Coogler, starring Michael B. Jordan in a dual role as twin brothers Elijah and Elias "Stack" Moore. Set in 1932 Mississippi, the story follows the brothers returning to their hometown to build a new life, only to face supernatural evil and racism in the Jim Crow South
